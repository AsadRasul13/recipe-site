Locations

'I truly love pasta' - A very wise person, probably

To achieve lasting happiness, all one needs is to know where the best pasta places are. To help guide you on this quest, here is a list of pasta places near you!

Pasta places in Central London

  1. Zizzis - There are truly Zizzis everywhere. Now you might feel overwhelmed by the choices, but you only need to pay attention to the pasta part of the menu. Honestly, why do they even include those other foods?
  2. Bella Italia - Another solid choice for all pasta lovers, as long as you keep your wits about you and don't accidentally order something else like a pizza
  3. Sainsbury's - If you're feeling like you need to make some pasta yourself, just go in to here and buy some ingredients to bring home!

Pasta places elsewhere

  1. Bella Italia Lancaster - Don't worry if you live in Lancaster, and consistently get pasta cravings! Our old friend Bella Italia has an outpost there as well, and can provide for all your pasta needs.
  2. La Bottega Siciliana, Moscow - If you're visiting the great city of Moscow, no need to fear - there is a pasta restaurant for you! With many ratings and many stars, you can't go wrong with this
  3. Gino's Spaghetti House, Oxford - All you Oxforders will be pleased to hear that some wise man Gino has provided you with a spaghetti house! Jubilations!
